## Signal Ranking

Highest concern deltas:

1. Rochester St. George: own-building parish with named clergy and weekly/weekday Lithuanian Mass became hosted mission/community status with TBD schedule.
2. Philadelphia St. Andrew: Lithuanian Mass persists, but resident Lithuanian pastor baseline became non-Lithuanian administrator/visiting-clergy dependence.
3. Brighton Park Immaculate Conception: Sunday Lithuanian Mass persists, but governance moved into a merged multiethnic parish and weekday Lithuanian Mass is no longer confirmed.
4. Cleveland St. Casimir: Lithuanian Mass and Lithuanian clergy persist, but two parish structures collapsed into one consolidated survivor.

Relative continuity:

1. Lemont Blessed Jurgis Matulaitis Mission: strongest continuity from Litua baseline to current state.
2. South Boston St. Peter: Mass-time continuity is strong, but clergy/governance changed materially.
3. Chicago Nativity BVM: Lithuanian liturgical life remains strong; current risk is financial/building-control exposure rather than loss of Lithuanian Mass.
